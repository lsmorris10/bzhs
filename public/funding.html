⚔ What the Investment Built

  • Zombie AI System — behavior trees, block breaking, wall occlusion, horde mechanics, night speed scaling
  • Survival Stats — hunger, thirst, stamina, temperature with environmental modifiers
  • Health & Debuffs — infection, bleeding, exhaustion, hypothermia/hyperthermia with persistence
  • HUD Overlay — stat bars, compass, minimap, day counter, all rendered in real-time
  • Loot & Crafting — custom items, recipes, textures, models, blockstates, language files
  • XP & Perks — progression system with unlockable abilities
  • Day/Night Cycle — doubled day length, sky rendering, Blood Moon events
  • Landing Site — marketing page, dev dashboard, deployment pipeline, self-hosted downloads
