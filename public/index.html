Brutal Zombie Horde Survival

Minecraft 1.21.4 — NeoForge — Alpha

A 7 Days to Die-inspired survival overhaul for Minecraft. Blood moons, zombie hordes, 45 perks, full crafting rework, and much more.

Install NeoForge 21.4.140 for Minecraft 1.21.4, then drag the .jar into your mods folder.

Key Features

🧟

18 Zombie Variants

Dual speed system: zombies get faster at night AND in darkness. Night bonus + light-level bonus (highest wins).

🌑

Blood Moon Hordes

Every 7th night brings a brutal horde event with escalating waves and no hiding.

💀

12 Survival Debuffs

Thirst, stamina, infection, bleeding, and more. Every decision matters.

🔨

Loot & Crafting Overhaul

Completely reworked crafting recipes, loot tables, and resource progression.

⭐

XP, Leveling & 45 Perks

Earn XP, level up, and unlock perks across combat, survival, and utility trees.

🧭

Full HUD System

Compass, minimap, and stats overlay keep you informed without breaking immersion.

🌅

Extended Day Cycle

Day/night cycle doubled to ~40 real minutes via slower-tick approach. Vanilla 24k time scale preserved.
